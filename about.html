ABOUT

Fofana Express Hair Braiding

We are an African braiding salon in the heart of Harlem at 611 Malcolm X Blvd. We specialize in neat, long-lasting protective styles and fast services in a warm, welcoming space.

OUR HISTORY

Rooted in Harlem.

We bring quality African braiding to the community. Over time, we have grown into a trusted neighborhood salon, known for our skills, creativity, and welcoming atmosphere. Our goal is to make every client feel confident, beautiful, and valued.

OUR TEAM

Skilled hands. Warm service.

Our braiders are passionate about their craft. We are committed to quality, care, and customer satisfaction.
